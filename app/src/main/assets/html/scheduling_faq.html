What if the scheduled time is no longer feasible?

Contact our customer service team as soon as possible and we will do our best to assist you.

 

How soon can I book my Service or cleaning?

The soonest you can book your service is 48 hours from the time that you book.

 

How does the cancellation process work?

If there are valid reasons requiring you to cancel the job you have requested, we will reimburse your payment as follows:

 

Cancellation :

More than 3 days before scheduled date. 100% of payment made

Less than 3 days and more than 1 working day before schedule 50% of payment made

Less than 1 day (24 hours) No Reimbursement

What if I am paying for another person and he/she is not home?

Our Handymen will be using the contact details that you have provided. If you are paying on another person’s behalf, let us know by adding a remark in the text box labelled “Add any further details", together with that person’s contact details.

Do I need to get permission from my building management before the Handymen/Cleaners show up?

It depends on your building. We strongly advise that you check with your apartment/condominium manager to determine whether the working permits is required for entry complete your booked service.
