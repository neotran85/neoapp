What does the general home cleaning session include?

Cleaning includes completion of the following primary services:

  • Cleaning of all bedrooms requested;
  • Cleaning of all bathrooms and toilets requested;
  • Cleaning of kitchen(s) requested;
  • Cleaning of living room and balcony (if applicable);
  • Cleaning includes: sweeping, mopping, dusting, tidying up, and vacuuming.

 

Additional services (if allocated hours permit) after primary services are completed:

  • Laundry;
  • Ironing;
  • Folding clothes;
  • Ceiling fan cleaning (not more than 8 ft high).

 

How much will a general home cleaning session cost?

Standard- 1 Cleaner/3 hours RM80

Standard with Cleaning Supplies- 1 Cleaner/3 hours RM105

Moving in/out Cleaning RM210

 

Can I request for other services out of the job scope?

Yes, you can. However, additional charges may apply. These requests have to be communicated in advance and mutual agreement must occur (in addition to job feasibility) before proceeding. A separate invoice maybe provided to you for said requests or the additional charges will be charged within the same invoice.


Will the Cleaner provide the cleaning supplies?

If the option is selected at booking the appointment the cleaner will come prepared with cleaning supplies, if this option is not selected upon booking you must supply cleaning supplies for the cleaner.

 

Can I request for additional hours if the number of hours booked are not enough?

You will have to purchase another cleaning session for another day. It is advisable that you purchase more hours and/or more cleaners per cleaning session if you require more work to be done or if your property is large.

 

What does the A/C servicing include?

The scope of A/C servicing is dependent upon the type of A/C (wall mount or ceiling mount) and type of service you have selected.

AppyService offers two types of AC cleaning services:

 

 

  • Standard Aircon Cleaning

 

  • Cleaning and checking of indoor filter
  • Cleaning of evaporator coil
  • Flushing of drainage system
  • Checking of A/C fan bearing
  • Test-run of the system to ensure it is in proper working condition
  • Cleaning of blower wheels and blades
  • Checking of thermostats and control R22 gas top up at no additional charge

 

 

  • Chemical Aircon Cleaning

 

  • Cleaning and checking of indoor filter
  • Cleaning of evaporator coil
  • Flushing of drainage system
  • Checking of A/C fan bearing
  • Test-run of the system to ensure it is in proper working condition
  • Chemical Cleaning of external and internal unit
  • Cleaning of blower wheels and blades
  • Checking of thermostats and control R22 gas top up at no additional charge

 

How much will an AC servicing session cost?

Standard Cleaning: RM120

Chemical Cleaning RM160

 

What should I do if my A/C requires repair?

If your aircon needs repair you will be notified by the AppyService’s Contractor prior to servicing your aircon, if you would like them to repair your Aircon you will be issued a quotation and invoice separately from the original order.

You are encouraged to contact our customer support so that the repairs and invoice can be recorded in our system in case
